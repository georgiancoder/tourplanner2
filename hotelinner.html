4 Star apart hotel Citadines Freedom Square Tbilisi is centrally located in the heart of Georgia’s capital, next to a popular tourist spot in Freedom Square. Cafes, restaurants and supermarkets are conveniently located within a few minutes’ walk. The Apart’hotel is in close vicinity to the State Chancellery and the House of Parliament.

Property has 66 air-conditioned modern apartments. Rooms are stylish and come with a flat-screen TV and DVD player. Kitchens come with a microwave, fridge and hot drinks facilities. A freshly prepared continental breakfast is served every morning.

Services include:

  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Free parking
  • 24-hour front desk
  • Private check-in/check-out
  • Luggage storage
  • Concierge service

Hotel has fitness centre with panoramic windows. After a workout here guests can enjoy a relaxing massage. Amenities also include a washing and drying machines.
